What is A.C.E?

A.C.E (Academic Companion Engine) is a personal academic assistant designed to help students manage their education, stay organised, and improve their learning experience.

Smart Organisation

Track assignments, deadlines, schedules, and tasks all in one place. A.C.E keeps you on top of everything with a smart dashboard and calendar system.

AI Learning Support

Get help understanding concepts, answer questions, and receive personalised study guidance from an AI assistant that adapts to your learning style.
