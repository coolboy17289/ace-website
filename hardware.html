Ecosystem

A.C.E is designed as more than a digital platform. It exists as a dedicated academic companion device built around accessible and affordable hardware — a personalised learning assistant students can use every day.

A.C.E Core Device

The foundation is a small single-board computer providing enough performance for the operating system, AI tools, and student applications. The device runs the full A.C.E software environment with direct access to planning tools, AI assistance, and academic management.

  • Raspberry Pi 5 (16GB RAM) primary system
  • Raspberry Pi 4 compatibility for lower-cost builds
  • Active cooling solution
  • USB-C power delivery
  • Built-in WiFi & Bluetooth
  • GPIO expansion for hardware modules

Interactive Student Display

A dedicated touchscreen interface allows students to interact naturally with their academic assistant. The screen acts as a central command centre, showing important information without requiring them to constantly open a laptop or phone.

  • 7-inch touchscreen display
  • Custom dashboard interface
  • Quick access to assignments & schedules
  • Touch-based controls for notes & planning
  • Real-time academic information display

Learning & Environment Sensors

Integrated sensors help A.C.E become aware of its surroundings and provide a more personalised, intelligent experience tailored to the student's study environment.

  • Camera module for AI vision features
  • Ambient light sensor for auto display adjustment
  • Temperature & humidity monitoring
  • RGB status lighting for notifications
  • Audio input/output (speakers & microphones)

Physical Design

The enclosure is designed to feel like a modern educational device — professional, practical, and built specifically for students.

  • Custom 3D-printed case
  • Hidden cable management
  • Integrated cooling vents
  • Secure Pi mounting & screen frame
  • Clean desktop-friendly design

Expandable Platform

A.C.E grows alongside the student. The modular hardware lets students and developers add new features over time through additional components.

  • Rotary encoder controls
  • Additional buttons or shortcuts
  • Advanced sensor modules
  • Custom accessories
  • Educational electronics kits

A Complete Physical & Digital Ecosystem

By combining dedicated hardware with intelligent software, A.C.E becomes a complete academic ecosystem. A personal companion that students can see, touch, and use every day — helping them stay organised, learn effectively, and build better academic habits.
